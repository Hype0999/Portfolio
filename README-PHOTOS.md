# 📸 How to Upload Your Photos

I have already updated the code so you do **not** need to edit any JavaScript files. 
To get your photos showing on the website instantly, just follow these two steps:

### 1. Rename your photos exactly like this:
Take your five main photos and rename them on your computer to:
- `stinky-shoes.jpg` (Project 1)
- `smart-bicycle.jpg` (Project 2)
- `pi-control.jpg` (Project 3)
- `smart-water-tank.jpg` (Project 4)
- `monsoon-labs.jpg` (This is the large image for the right side of the Monsoon Labs section)

*(Make sure they are `.jpg` files! If you are using `.png`, you will need to convert them or rename the extensions in the code).*

### 2. Move them into the right folder:
1. Open the `public` folder in your project.
2. Inside `public`, create a new folder named `assets` (if it doesn't exist).
3. Drag and drop all five of your renamed photos directly into the `public/assets/` folder.

That's it! As soon as you put those renamed files into that folder, they will automatically appear on the Project Cards and inside the Case Studies. Your work is completely done. 🚀
